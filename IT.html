IT INTEREST

What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT Start?

I am interested in all parts of IT, from the software/developing program side to the hardware side of IT such as computer parts, building and setting up systems/networks. Although I prefer more of the hardware side rather than the software as coding can be a pain sometimes. My interest in IT has always stuck with me since I was young. It started from console games that I used to play when I was younger on the PS3 and eventually I transitioned into PC games and from that point on, I've just been genuinely interested in technology. My computer was also the only thing that I would use mostly, so IT just stuck out to me compared to any other path.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?

The reason why I chose to come to RMIT specifically was because of what it had to offer. For convenience and also because it aligned with the career path of IT. I had originally wanted to go to Victoria University in Footscray Park as they also offered something similar and was closer but I chose RMIT after hearing and asking around, to which a lot of people had said they also had attended this same university. Afterwards I changed my first preference to RMIT, with the thought that the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology would surely offer the best when it comes to technology.

What do you expect to learn during your studies?

During my time at RMIT, I hope to acquire and improve on some current skills. These include teamwork, problem-solving and developing my understanding of the inner workings of technology and it can impact the world around us. I expect to learn how to program and code at a better level, if possible, learn a variety of programming languages along the way, what is required and expected within the IT industry learning about the many paths of IT that can be pursued. While doing that I also wish to improve on my communication skills such as collaborating in group projects, seeking advice from my fellow IT peers and to converse effectively with clients.
